::question{:type="questionType"}
What is the significance of v-once?

::

::explanation{:type="explanationType"}

**Explanation: v-once is used to render an element or component only once, and skip future updates**

<br />

```js
<div v-once>
  <h1>Comment</h1>
  <p>Hello world</p>
</div>
```
